OUR FEATURES

SELF-FILLING WATER TANKS

Off-the-Grid’s self-sustaining water tanks operate using Atmospheric Water Generators (AWGs). By extracting moisture from humidity inside and outside of the box, each system is able to generate enough water on its own to feed the crops.

Renewable Energy Source

Attached to each box are solar panels and windmills to help power electricity without being attached to the grid. By harnessing renewable resources such as wind and solar energy, Off-the-Grid helps customers reduce their carbon footprint, lower their energy bill and eliminate the potential for lost crops due to blackouts.

Robotic Assistants

Off-the-Grid autonomous growing systems operate using advanced robotic technology. We place machine-controlled arms within each box to carry out agricultural tasks such as planting, trimming and harvesting crops.

Powered by IOS or Android

With the purchase of each Off-the-Grid unit, Box Holders will receive access to a proprietary InGrown Organics software. Compatible with IOS or Android, users can use their mobile app to select crops based on detailed analytics and grow them remotely from the palm of their hand.

Easy shipping with Ingrown Organics

As a Box Holder, you’ll receive access to InGrown Organics’ premier distribution network designed to assist growers in distributing their crops. Through this program, customers can rely on our team to facilitate sales between retail stores, wholesalers, hotels, restaurants, dispensaries and independent resellers. In addition to same-day or next-day shipping, box holders will also receive detailed analytics offering bespoke growing guidance based on their specific sales and location.
